Pronouns

Did you go out to play last evening?

Suppose you played with your friend John, now can we say John is a noun?

Yes, John is a noun because it is the name of a person. But what if I replace “John” with some other word? What will you write?

Well, we can use “He” in place of John because John is a boy.

For example: John is a good boy.
We can also write it as “He is a good boy.”

We replaced “John” (Noun) with the word “He” (Pronoun).
